FACEBOOK GLOBAL OUTAGE 2021

On 4 October 2021, Facebook experienced a global outage that affected all of its major platforms: not only Facebook, but also Instagram, WhatsApp, and Messenger for several hours! This incident highlighted how a single internal network change can have cascading effects across multiple services worldwide, demonstrating the fragility and interdependence of large-scale digital infrastructures.
