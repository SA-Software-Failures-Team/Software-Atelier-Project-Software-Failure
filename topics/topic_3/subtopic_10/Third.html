Equifax Data Breach by the numbers


These are the data from the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) and MarketWatch. The cyber attack exposed personal information of about 147 million American people, almost half of the country's population. The stolen information includes names, birth dates, Social Security numbers, addresses, and also phone numbers, emails, driver's licenses, and credit cards.
