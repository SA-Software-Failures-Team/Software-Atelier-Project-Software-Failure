Equifax Shares Drop 31%


In September 2017, Equifax stock price following the disclosure of one of the largest data breaches in history. The company's shares fell fastly from $142.72 to $98.99, representing a 31% drop and wiping out approximately $5 billion in market capitalization within days. The market reaction showed that a cyberattack can quickly destroy trust in a company, even a big firm like Equifax lost quickly its good reputation! Rebuilding that trust took time, honesty and obviously...stronger data security!
