TSB Bank (2018)

In 2018, TSB Bank in the United Kingdom faced one of the most significant IT failures in modern banking history. During an ambitious system migration intended to give the bank full control of its technology, a critical software malfunction left millions of customers unable to access their accounts, transfer money, or make payments for weeks. The following pages examine the event in detail — exploring how the failure unfolded, the steps taken to resolve it, and the financial and social consequences that reshaped both TSB’s operations and industry standards for operational resilience.
