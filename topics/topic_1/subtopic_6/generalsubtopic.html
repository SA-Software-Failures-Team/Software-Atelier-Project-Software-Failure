The Boeing 737 MAX Crisis: A Software Vulnerability with Global Implications

The grounding of the Boeing 737 MAX fleet following two fatal crashes in late 2018 and early 2019 exposed a catastrophic failure rooted in the aircraft's new flight control software, the Maneuvering Characteristics Augmentation System (MCAS). This event went far beyond a simple technical malfunction, revealing deep-seated issues in design philosophy, corporate culture, and regulatory oversight.
