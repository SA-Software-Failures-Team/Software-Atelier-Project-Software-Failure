Aerospace and Transportation
Transport and aerospace are vital to global stability, enabling trade, communication, and mobility across nations. As these sectors increasingly rely on complex software systems, their efficiency and safety depend on technological precision and reliability. A single failure—whether due to technical malfunction or cyberattack—can disrupt supply chains, halt transportation networks, and endanger lives. Ensuring the resilience and security of these systems is therefore essential to protect both economic continuity and human safety worldwide.
