F22 Flight Control Bug - Timeline

Timeline of the software bug:

  • Day 0: Aircraft enters service, initial testing complete.
  • Month 3: First minor control anomalies observed during simulations.
  • Month 6: Bug detected in flight control algorithms handling sensor input.
  • Month 7: Patch developed and deployed to test fleet.
  • Month 8: Full operational deployment resumes after verification.

Lessons Learned / Prevention

- Rigorous testing of sensor input handling is critical.
- Simulation must include edge cases and long-duration flights.
- Small software bugs can propagate and lead to safety issues.
- Continuous monitoring and patch deployment are essential.
