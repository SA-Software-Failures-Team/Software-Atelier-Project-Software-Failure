The attack: Details


Right before the attack

23rd December 2015, this is the day the attackers found the VPN keys. With these keys they could access the Supervisory Control and Data Acquisition (SCADA) systems. This meant that the attackers had remote access to the power grids, and there was no going back from here. There was no need for hiding anymore.

The attack

The attackers started sending commands to turn power grids off. 30 stations lost electricity and 230,000 people were left in the dark.

While this was happening, the attackers did a TdoS attack. This meant that the call centres of the power companies were spammed with fake calls. This made it impossible for real people to communicate with the company, stopping the companies from knowing the scale of this attack.

After this, the KillDisk malware was dropped.
