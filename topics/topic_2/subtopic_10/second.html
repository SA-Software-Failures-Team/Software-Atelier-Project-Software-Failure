The Oldsmar Water Treatment Facility

The Oldsmar Water Treatment Facility, located in Oldsmar, Florida, is responsible for treating and distributing clean water to thousands of residents. The facility uses industrial control systems (ICS) and SCADA software to manage water purification processes, including the precise dosing of chemicals such as sodium hydroxide and chlorine.


Operators remotely monitor water quality and chemical levels to ensure public safety. While these systems allow for efficient management, they also create potential entry points for cyber intrusions if not properly secured. Before the attack, remote access was permitted through standard protocols without multi-factor authentication, making it vulnerable to exploitation.
