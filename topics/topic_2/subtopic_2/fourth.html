Bug vs Vulnerability

On a final look at the shut-down, was it actually just a bug or a vulnerability that was exploited intentionally or unintentionally?



In my opinion, the answer is that it was both, but the bug is what triggered the shutdown.

Vulnerability:

The lack of a firewall was a vulnerability, that was somehow exploited, allowing the bug to exist in the operation network.

Bug:

The software update was a bug. This bug could travel to the operation network to cause the reset and reboot because of the vulnerability.

Conclusion: The bad design, a vulnerability allowed the problem to occur, but the software bug was the cause of the shutdown.

Aftermath

The cyberattack caused a lot of damage. Not only did people have to survive without power in the winter cold, but more importantly, people got something to be scared of. The reign of cyber warfare started.

This type of warfare is not entirely about hackers and malware, but also a psychological warfare. The biggest Vulnerability that was exploited was the humanity and fear.
