Bug vs Vulnerability

On a final look at the shut-down, was it actually just a bug or a vulnerability that was exploited intentionally or unintentionally? In my opinion, the answer is that it was both, but the bug is what triggered the shutdown.

Vulnerability:
The lack of a firewall was a vulnerability, that was somehow exploited, allowing the bug to exist in the operation network.

Bug:
The software update was a bug. This bug could travel to the operation network to cause the reset and reboot because of the vulnerability.

Conclusion:
The bad design, a vulnerability allowed the problem to occur, but the software bug was the cause of the shutdown.
