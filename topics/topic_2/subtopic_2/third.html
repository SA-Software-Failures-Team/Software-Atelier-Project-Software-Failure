How this affected the future of cyber-security.


As we saw in the previous part, the software update is a mistake. One that can happen to everyone. However, the main question is that this kind of mistake should not happen as “everyday” mistakes should be anticipates and avoided.

This meant authorities would have to not only check outside for threats, but also inside. As the easiest way for a cyber-attack in power-plants may have been to work for the power-plant.

A primary failure point, of this event, was the bad decision to allow the corporate networks and operative networks to coexist without proper boundaries.

This incident brought a bigger problem than just technical settings. A small bug causing a whole plant to shut-down, means that the whole system is not properly working. This means every single employee on the power-plant have a lot of power.. Anyone with access, has the ability to cause a lot of damage, accidentally or on purpose.
