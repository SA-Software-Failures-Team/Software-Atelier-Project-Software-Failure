Infection Statistics

Symantec recorded over 100,000 infected hosts in more than 150 countries, with the majority located in Iran. Other affected regions included Indonesia and India.


Why Stuxnet Was Unique First malware known to physically alter industrial processes. Used four zero-day vulnerabilities and legitimate digital signatures. Highly targeted design focused on a single industrial process.


Key Lessons for Cybersecurity Segment and monitor industrial control networks. Implement strict USB and removable media policies. Patch vulnerabilities promptly and limit admin privileges. Monitor PLC logic integrity through regular offline audits.

Long-term Influence Stuxnet marked the beginning of the cyber-physical warfare era, inspiring new research and stronger defenses in critical infrastructure protection.
