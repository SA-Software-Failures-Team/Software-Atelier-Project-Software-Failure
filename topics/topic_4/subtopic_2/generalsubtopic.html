Panama City Radiotherapy Accident (2000)

In 2000, a series of overdoses in a radiotherapy center in Panama City led to severe patient injuries and fatalities. The incident revealed critical gaps in equipment calibration, procedural oversight, and safety culture in radiotherapy practices.
