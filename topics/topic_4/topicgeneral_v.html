Healthcare
In this topic, we investigate cybersecurity vulnerabilities that have exposed healthcare systems to attacks and data breaches. With hospitals and medical devices increasingly connected to the internet, weaknesses in their software can jeopardize patient safety and privacy. By examining notable incidents, we will analyze how these vulnerabilities were exploited, their impact on healthcare operations, and the security strategies needed to build resilience in digital health infrastructure.
WannaCry Ransomware - NHS (May 2017)
U.S. Medical Imaging Servers Exposed (2019)
