In this topic, we will examine the critical role of software in healthcare, where reliability can mean the difference between life and death. We will explore cases where software bugs or vulnerabilities in medical devices, diagnostic tools, and life-support systems have directly threatened patient safety. By analyzing the causes, consequences, and lessons learned from these incidents, we aim to highlight the importance of rigorous software engineering practices to ensure safe and effective healthcare technology.
