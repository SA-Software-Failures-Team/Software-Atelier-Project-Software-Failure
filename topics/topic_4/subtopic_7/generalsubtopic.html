US Medical Imaging Servers Exposed (2019): When Health Data Went Public

In 2019, a shocking discovery revealed that thousands of American patients had their medical scans freely accessible on the internet. It wasn’t the result of a hacker attack or a sophisticated breach, but rather a chain of missteps, outdated systems, and overlooked configurations. Sensitive medical data — names, birth dates, even diagnoses — were left floating in the open web for anyone to find.
