UK COVID-19 Excel Data Loss (2020)

In autumn 2020, the UK's pandemic response was severely compromised by a catastrophic software failure: the loss of 15,841 positive COVID-19 test results due to reliance on a Microsoft Excel spreadsheet for critical national data aggregation. This "Excel blunder" exposed dangerous flaws in public health infrastructure and serves as a stark warning about using inappropriate tools in high-stakes environments.
