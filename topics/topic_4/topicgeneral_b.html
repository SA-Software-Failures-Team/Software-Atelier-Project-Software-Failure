Healthcare
In this topic, we delve into how software bugs in healthcare systems can have serious, sometimes life-threatening consequences. From programming errors in medical devices to flawed hospital information systems, even minor mistakes can lead to major disruptions in patient care. Through real-world cases, we will uncover how these bugs arose, what went wrong in testing and design, and what measures can prevent such failures in future healthcare technologies.
