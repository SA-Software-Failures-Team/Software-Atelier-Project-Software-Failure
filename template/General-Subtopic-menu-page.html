Microsoft Azure / Office 365 Outage (2023)

On 25 January 2023, Microsoft experienced a significant disruption affecting Microsoft 365 services (including Exchange Online, Teams, OneDrive) and its underlying Azure network infrastructure. The outage illustrates how even the largest cloud providers are vulnerable to changes in their internal infrastructure and the cascading effects on many dependent services.
