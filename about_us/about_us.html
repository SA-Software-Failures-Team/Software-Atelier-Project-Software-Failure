Transportation

Leader: Skaria
Members: Roscio, Germann Vieira, Brinjevec, Coutinho

Transport and aerospace are vital to global stability, enabling trade, communication, and mobility across nations. As these sectors increasingly rely on complex software systems, their efficiency and safety depend on technological precision and reliability. Ensuring the resilience and security of these systems is therefore essential to protect both economic continuity and human safety worldwide.



Energy / Infrastructure

Leader: Rissone
Members: Santagostino, Delipetrev, Cocchiara, Shirkani, Chatterjee

Energy and infrastructure systems are the backbone of modern society, powering everything from grids to water plants. A single software failure — be it a bug or cyberattack — can trigger blackouts, disrupt essential services, and threaten national security. Protecting this digital infrastructure is vital for societal survival.



Business-Critical Systems

Leader: Scapellato
Members: Bertolini, D’arrigo, Calvello, Bianchi, Poopalan

Business-critical systems support essential organizational functions like finance, logistics, and customer service. Their failure can cause major financial loss or reputational harm. Ensuring reliability and recovery capability is crucial for business continuity.



Healthcare

Leader: Ricetti
Members: Albonico, Spanò, Lazarevic, Wilinski

Healthcare technology is central to patient safety, from diagnostics to life-support systems. Software bugs or vulnerabilities are direct threats to human lives — a single error can result in misdiagnosis or treatment failure. Reliability here is a matter of life and death.
